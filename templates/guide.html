Website Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Franklin's Timer! To begin, you need to create an account or log in if you already have one.

Creating an Account

  1. Click on the "Register" link in the navigation bar.
  2. Fill in the required fields: username and password (16-character limit).
  3. Click the blue "Register" button to create your account.

Logging In

  1. Click on the "Login" link in the navigation bar.
  2. Enter your username and password.
  3. Click "Login" to gain access to full functionality of this website.

Creating Sessions

After logging in, you will be redirected to the Sessions page:

  1. If you don't have any sessions, enter a session name of your liking.
  2. Click "Create Session" to create the session.
  3. Click the white button with the session name to select that session, and you will be redirected to the timer page.
  4. Click the red "Delete" button to delete a session and all solves in that session.

Using the Timer

Once you have selected a session, you can start timing your Rubik's Cube solves:

  1. Follow the scramble at the top of the screen and match your cube with the displayed image to make sure it is correct.
  2. Press and release the space bar to start the timer and begin solving.
  3. Press the space bar to stop the timer, this is to ensure accurate timing.
  4. A new scramble is generated and the scramble display image updates once the timer has stopped.
  5. On the right side of the screen, your selected session, current AO5, and the most recent 5 solves will be displayed.
  6. If you wish to delete the most recent solve in your current session, click the "Delete Most Recent Solve" button below the timer.

    7. *Note: AO5 stands for Average of 5 in Rubik's Cube terminology. It is calculated by removing the fastest and the slowest solves in 5 solves and then take the mean of the rest of the solves.

Viewing Results

After completing a solve, you can view your results:

  1. Click the "Results" button on the navigation bar.
  2. Here, you can see a list of all your solves in your selected session, along with scramble information and times.
  3. Use the "Sort by:" dropdown to change the order your solves are sorted in.
  4. You can also click on individual solves for more details.

Deleting Solves

If you need to remove a solve:

  1. Go to the "Results" page.
  2. Click the "Delete Most Recent Solve" button towards the top to remove your last solve.
  3. Click the "Delete All Solves" button to remove all solves in the session.
  4. Click on the "See Stats" button of a specific solve, then click on the "Delete This Solve" button to remove an individual solve.

Change Login Details

If you would like to change your login details:

  1. Click the "Profile" button in the navigation bar.
  2. You may change your username or password by typing in the required details.
  3. You may delete you account by clickign the "Delete My Account" button on the bottom.

Logging Out

When you're finished, remember to log out:

  1. Click the "Logout" button in the navigation bar.
  2. You will be redirected to the home page with all your information securely stored.

    3. *Note: You don't need to worry about the safety of your information, all form of cache is prevented and no users can access your information after you log out
